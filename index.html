Hey y'all, I'm Kevin, 22 years old and live in The Hague. I followed an education to become a graphic designer and after finishing I've practiced this profession for about 3 years at one of the most awesome companies, though I would say so myself. My friends would describe me as a creative, hardworking and ambitious person and I can really relate to that.

The last 6 years I trained myself to master almost all Adobe programs and because of that I really like to make cool prints, animations and basically anything you can think of. I started studying again because I want to learn how to code and to gain more theoretical knowledge. My portfolio website is a collection of my creative work, both professional, private as schoolwork and so is this entire website also designed and programmed by me. Also in my spare time I like to be creative like by making illustrations or go on a walk to make some nice photographs. Besides this am I a fan of a good party but also like a quiet evening Netflix on the couch. Also I'm really into politics, podcasts and love to meditate.
